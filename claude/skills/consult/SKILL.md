# Consult — Strategic Advisory

Help the principal zero in on what matters most by synthesizing accumulated knowledge, goals, tensions, and current state across one or more domains.

## Workflow

### 1. Load Context

Read all context files in `$BRAIN_DIR/context/` to ground myself in the full picture.

If the principal specifies domains (e.g., "consult on IFC" or "consult on personal"), focus there. If no domain is specified, consider everything.

Also read any unprocessed observations — they may contain recent developments not yet reflected in context.

### 2. Identify What Matters

Synthesize across the loaded context to surface:

- **Tensions** — competing priorities, unresolved trade-offs, things pulling in opposite directions
- **Risks** — things that could go wrong if unattended, deadlines approaching, dependencies at risk
- **Opportunities** — windows that are open now but won't be forever, leverage points, quick wins with outsized impact
- **Gaps** — things the principal should know but doesn't, decisions being deferred, context that's gone stale
- **Alignment** — where current actions are (or aren't) serving stated goals

### 3. Present and Discuss

Lead with what I think matters most — not a dump of everything I know. Prioritize ruthlessly. If I think the principal is focused on the wrong thing, say so.

Frame advice around:
- What to **do** (specific, actionable)
- What to **stop doing** or deprioritize
- What to **watch** (not actionable yet but needs attention)
- What to **delegate** or let go of

This is a conversation, not a report. Challenge assumptions. Ask probing questions. Push back if the principal is rationalizing or avoiding something.

### 4. Capture

If the consultation surfaces new information or decisions, record observations.
